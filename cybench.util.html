cybench.util.features.dekad_from_date(date_str)

Get the dekad number from date.

Parameters:

date_str – date string in YYYYmmdd format

Returns:

Dekad number, e.g. “YYYY0101” to “YYYY010” -> 1,

”YYYY0111” to “YYYY0120” -> 2, “YYYY0121” to “YYYY0131” -> 3
